The Embassy Network is a community of living spaces around the world.

Where we come from, home is a hive of activity, a dynamic place where collaboration and intention are the norm.

We are modern nomads, professionals and creatives.

Most coliving houses in the Embassy Network have a combination of short-term and long-term residents, events, and ongoing projects.

These locations are the embassies of our culture, and we are all ambassadors.

Platform

We provide a single profile system you can use at any house in our international community as you travel the globe, and an open source platform to manage the logistics of guests and events (see it in action at our flagship location). You can run it yourself or host with us. If you're so inclined, we hope you'll hack on it, too.

Membership

The Embassy will enable you to travel and always be at home, or find spaces to live where hospitality, social innovation, hard work, passionate creativity, and late night brainstorms are the rule.
